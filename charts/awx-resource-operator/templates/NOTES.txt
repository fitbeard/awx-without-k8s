⚠️  RECONCILE MODEL — fire-and-forget (NOT a continuous reconciler):
   Each CR runs ONCE. After the spawned runner Job sets
   status.isFinished=true, every subsequent reconcile hits an
   `end_play` early-exit. Spec edits to the CR are silently ignored.
   AWX-side drift is NOT corrected.

   To force a re-run:
     kubectl delete <cr> && kubectl apply -f <cr>     # or
     kubectl patch <cr> --subresource=status --type=merge \
       -p '{"status":{"isFinished":false}}'
